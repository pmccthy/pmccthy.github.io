Welcome! I'm excited to launch this new website to share my research, projects, and ongoing thoughts with the broader community.

## What You'll Find Here

**[Projects]({% link projects.md %})** — A selection of my current and past research projects, including descriptions of research questions, methodologies, and outcomes.

**[Blog]({% link blog.md %})** — Occasional posts about research methodology, tools, interesting findings, and reflections on academic life.

**[About]({% link about.md %})** — Background information about my research interests and academic journey.

## Why Start a Research Website?

As a PhD student preparing for the academic job market (and internships!), I wanted to create a space where potential collaborators, hiring committees, and interested readers can easily learn about my work. This site serves as:

1. **A research portfolio** — Showcasing my projects and publications in one organized place
2. **A communication platform** — Sharing thoughts and insights through blog posts
3. **A professional presence** — A clean, accessible academic website
